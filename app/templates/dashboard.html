Category Descriptions

Responsiveness

How quickly and smoothly the team delivers working software. Covers release automation, refinement practices, and release frequency.

Continuous Improvement

The team's ability to learn and adapt. Includes quality mindset, retrospective effectiveness, learning culture, psychological safety, and use of metrics.

Stakeholders

How well the team collaborates with stakeholders. Covers sprint goals clarity, review quality, ongoing collaboration, and value focus.

Team Effectiveness

Overall team health and output quality. Includes morale, stakeholder satisfaction, and perceived value delivery.

Team Autonomy

The team's ability to self-organize and make decisions independently. Covers self-management and cross-functionality.

Management Support

How well management enables the team. Focuses on removing blockers, shielding from distractions, and providing resources.
